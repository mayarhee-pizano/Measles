About Measles

What is the Measles?

The measles is an respitory disease that can spread quickly and in some cases can be deadly.

Symptoms

Symptoms of the measles may include.

  • Rashes
  • Fever
  • Coughing
  • Runny Nose
  • Red Eyes

It is important to visit a doctor if you experience all of these symptoms

How long does it take for symptoms to show?

Symptoms of the measles usually start to show 10-12 days after being exposed to the disease.

How does the Measles spread?

The measles disease can be spread by coming in contact with an infected person's cough or sneeze
