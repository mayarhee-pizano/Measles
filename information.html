What is the Measles?

The measles is an respitory disease that can spread quickly and in some cases can be deadly.

Symptoms

Symptoms of the measles may include.

  • Rashes
  • Fever
  • Coughing
  • Runny Nose
  • Red Eyes

It is important to visit a doctor if you experience all of these symptoms
